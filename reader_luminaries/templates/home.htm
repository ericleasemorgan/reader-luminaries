Reader Luminaries: Truth, Virtue, and Other Great Ideas

Reader Luminaries is a tool to supplement the reading of three pre-selected books:

  1. Works by Horace
  2. Essays by Francis Bacon
  3. The Prince by Machiavelli

Through the use of this tool your understanding of these three books will be enhanced.

Think of Reader Lite as if it were a traditional back-of-the-book index but made better with a set of super powers.

Reader Lite tool was written by me, Eric Lease Morgan <eric_morgan@infomotions.com>. As a librarian I have always been fascinated with the collection, organization, preservation, and dissemination of data, information, and knowledge. Reader Lite is simply another application in that vein.
