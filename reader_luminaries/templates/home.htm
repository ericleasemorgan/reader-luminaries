Reader Luminaries: Truth, Virtue, and Other Great Ideas

Reader Luminaries is a tool to supplement the reading of three pre-selected books:

  1. Essays by Francis Bacon
  2. The Prince by Machiavelli
  3. Works by Horace

Through the use of this tool your understanding of these three books will be enhanced. Think of Reader Luminaries as if it were a traditional back-of-the-book index but made better with a set of super powers.

Reader Luminaries was written by me, Eric Lease Morgan <eric_morgan@infomotions.com>. As a librarian I have always been fascinated with the collection, organization, preservation, and dissemination of data, information, and knowledge. Reader Luminaries is simply another application in that vein.
